Mini Pumpkin Cranberry Bread

Indulge your taste buds in the delightful world of mini pumpkin cranberry bread, a treat that blends the warm essence of pumpkin with the tart sweetness of cranberries. These mini loaves make them the perfect snack for festive gatherings. The rich, golden-brown crust gives way to a moist and tender interior. Each bite sweet and tart.

Ingredients

  1. 3 cups of flour
  2. 1 tbsp. pumpkin pie sauce
  3. 2 tbsp. baking soda
  4. 1-1/2 tsp. salt
  5. 3 cups of sugar
  6. 1 - 15 oz. can of libby's 100% pure pumpkin
  7. 4 large eggs
  8. 1 cup of vegetable oil
  9. 1/2 cup of orange juice
  10. 1 cup sweetened dried cranberries

Directions

Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Combine the flour, pumpkin pie spice, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl.

Then, combine the sugar, pure pumpkin, eggs, oil, and juice in a small bowl. Beat until just blended. Add the pumpkin mixture to the flour. Fold in the cranberries.

Separate into five loaf pans that are 5x5x3 inches. Bake at 350 degrees for 50 to 55 minutes.
