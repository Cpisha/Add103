Banana Bread

Get ready to embark on a culinary adventure with this amazing banana bread recipe! This easy-to-follow guide will help you create a mouthwatering treat that's perfect for breakfast or a tasty snack. The result? A moist and flavorful banana bread that will have your taste buds doing a happy dance.

Ingredients

  1. 3/4 cups of butter or margarine
  2. 1-1/2 cups of sugar
  3. 1-1/2 cups of ripe, smashed bananas
  4. 2 eggs
  5. 1 tsp. of vanilla
  6. 2 cups of flour
  7. 1 tsp. of baking soda
  8. 1 tsp. of salt
  9. 1/2 cup of sour milk

Directions

Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C). Make sure the oven rack is in the middle position. Grease a 9x5-inch (23x13 cm) loaf pan with butter or cooking spray. Peel the bananas and place them in a large bowl. Use a fork or potato masher to mash them until mostly smooth.

Melt the butter in the microwave or on the stove. Add the melted butter, vanilla extract, and eggs to the mashed bananas. Mix well with a spoon. Add both granulated sugar and brown sugar to the banana mixture. Stir until the sugar is well combined. In a separate bowl, mix the flour, baking soda, and a pinch of salt together. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the banana mixture, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

Pour the batter into the greased loaf pan, spreading it evenly. Place the pan in the preheated oven and bake for about 60-65 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow the banana bread to cool in the pan for 10-15 minutes, then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.
