Barbecued Brisket

Barbecued brisket of beef is a mouthwatering dish that takes beef to a whole new level of deliciousness. Imagine a thick cut of beef, seasoned to perfection with a blend of spices. This flavorful slab is then slow-cooked, allowing the meat to soak in the barbecue. The result? A tender, juicy brisket with a crispy, caramelized exterior that's simply irresistible.

Ingredients

    Beef

  1. 1 - three lb. beef brisket
  2. 1/2 tsp. salt
  3. 1/2 tsp. black pepper
  4. 1 tsp. paprika
  5. 1 sliced onion

    Barbecue Sauce

  1. 2 tbs. of shortening
  2. 1 chopped onion
  3. 2 tbs. cider vinegar
  4. 2 tbs. brown sugar
  5. 1 cup of ketchup
  6. 1 tbs. worcestershire sauce
  7. 1/2 cup hot water
  8. 1 tsp. dry mustard
  9. 1 tsp. salt

Directions

Cook the beef at 275 degrees in tightly covered Dutch oven for four hours. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and paprika. Put onion slices around the meat before roasting. Set aside while making the barbecue sauce.

Saute the onion in shortening in a saucepan. Add all the other ingredients except for the salt. Cook slowly for 30 minutes. Add the salt to taste at the end.

Slice the beef and put in a casserole dish. Pour the sauce over the meat and bake at 325 degrees until hot.
