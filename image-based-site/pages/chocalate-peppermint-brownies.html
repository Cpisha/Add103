Chocolate Peppermint Brownies

Chocolate-Peppermint Brownies are a delightful dessert that combines the rich flavors of chocolate with a refreshing hint of peppermint. These brownies are like a sweet symphony for your taste buds. They start with a dense, chocolatey base that's both soft and chewy, making each bite a heavenly experience.

But what sets these brownies apart is the touch of peppermint. It's like a cool breeze on a warm summer day. The peppermint swirls through the chocolate, adding a refreshing and minty zing that awakens your senses. It's not too overpowering; it's just right.

Topped with a sprinkling of crushed peppermint candies, these brownies are a visual and taste sensation. They're perfect for sharing with friends or savoring all by yourself. With every bite, you'll be transported to a world of deliciousness that's hard to resist. Chocolate-Peppermint Brownies are the perfect treat for anyone who loves the combination of sweet and minty goodness.

Ingredients

  1. 2 sticks unsalted butter, cut into small pieces, plus more for greasing
  2. 1 pound bittersweet chocolate, chopped
  3. 2 teaspoons pure peppermint extract
  4. 4 large eggs
  5. 1 3/4 cups packed light brown sugar
  6. 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  7. 1 teaspoon fine sea salt
  8. 4 candy canes, crushed (1/3 cup)

Directions

Preheat the oven to 350Â°. Butter a 9-by-13-inch baking pan and line with parchment paper; allow 2 inches of overhang on the long sides.

In a heatproof bowl, combine two-thirds of the chopped chocolate with the 2 sticks of butter. Set the bowl over a pot of simmering water and stir until melted. Scrape the chocolate into another bowl and let cool slightly. Add the remaining chopped chocolate and the peppermint extract to the heatproof bowl and melt over the simmering water; remove from the heat and let cool slightly.

In a medium bowl, whisk the eggs with the brown sugar until combined. Whisk in the chocolate-butter mixture until glossy and thick. Sprinkle the flour and salt into the bowl and stir until just incorporated. Spread the brownie batter in the prepared baking pan. Dollop the peppermint chocolate onto the brownie batter and swirl in with a table knife.

Bake the brownies in the center of the oven for 15 minutes. Sprinkle the crushed candy canes on top and bake for 10 to 15 minutes longer, until the edges are set and a toothpick inserted in the center comes out with a few moist crumbs. Let the brownies cool in the pan for at least 2 hours. Cut into squares and serve.
