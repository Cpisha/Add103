Ingredients

Salad

  1. 1/2 cup sliced almonds
  2. 3 T. sugar
  3. 1/2 head of iceberg lettuce
  4. 1/2 head of romaine lettuce
  5. 1 cup chopped celery
  6. 2 whole green onions, chopped
  7. 2 mandarin oranges
  8. 1/2 cup of raisins if desired

Dressing

  1. 1/2 tsp. salt
  2. 1/4 cup light olive oil
  3. 1 T. chopped parsley
  4. 2 T. apple cider vinegar
  5. A dash of tabasco sauce if desired

Directions

In a small pan over low heat, cook almonds and sugar till almonds are coated, and sugar is dissolved. Place the almonds in a cool location and store them in an airtight container.

Mix the lettuce, celery, and onions. Add the almonds, oranges, and dressing just before serving.
